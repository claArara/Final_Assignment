About

TOMORROW X TOGETHER (pronounced Tomorrow By Together, abbreviated as TXT) is a Korean boygroup under BigHit Music. Conformed by members Soobin, Yeonjun, Beomgyu, Taehyun and Hueningkai, the group made their debut in 2019 with their first EP The Dream Chapter: STAR, and have since captured the attention of millions of fans around the world.

Their songs cover a wide range of music genres, including K-Pop, Pop, Rock, R&B, Hip Hop and House. TXT's music tells the story about five boys who are brought together by a single dream. Their albums are divided into chapters, each conveying a series of emotions and events the protagonists of the story go through.
